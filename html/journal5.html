Using Portfolio

The Portfolio...

Now that you have mastered "starring" Journal entries and adding descriptive text in the Journal detail view, it is time to introduce the Portfolio activity.

1. Launch Portfolio from the Home View. You should see a slide show of your starred activities.
2. Try editing the description. Or add some comments.
3. Use the Save to PDF button on the main toolbar to save your portfolio to the Journal as a PDF that can be shared with a teacher or parent (or archived as part of the child's record of progress: not just what they know, but also, what they can do!!).

Are you ready to learn about the Portfolio?

The Detail View

Click on the "detail view" button on the far right-side of the listing for the document you created with the Write activity in an early task. It will take you to the Detail View.

In the Detail View, you find a preview image, and a place for adding descriptions, tags, and comments.

Add some text to the description of this entry. And mark it as important by clicking on the star icon next to the activity title.

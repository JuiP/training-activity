Exploring the toolbars

I see you stopped and restarted the activity. Well done. Notice that the activity remembered where you where when you left and restores that state when you restart.

Now, let's explore the View toolbar.

Hit the View button (second from the left). It will open a submenu with tools to modify the view, e.g., change the size of the graphics.

Once you have it open, you can continue to the next task.

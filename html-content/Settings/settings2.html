Changing the Sugar Nickname and Colours

When you started Sugar for the very first time, you were asked to provide a nickname and choose some colours.

Maybe you're still happy with them, maybe you're not.

In this task, you're going to find the Sugar settings, the About Me section, change your nickname and colours – then restart Sugar so that your changes take effect. Finally you'll open the One Academy Activity again so that you can continue with your learning!

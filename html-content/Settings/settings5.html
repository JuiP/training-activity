Other Settings: Switch Desktop

Pressing Switch Desktop in the settings menu will ask you whether you'd like to swap from the Sugar learning environement to the Gnome desktop environment.

What's Gnome?

It's a Linux desktop environment similar to Windows or OSX. Gnome on an XO comes equipped with an Internet browser, word processor and various other tools you might find useful.

If you ever end up there and want to get back to Sugar, just double click the Switch to Sugar icon on the desktop, or go to Applications > System Tools > Switch to Sugar in the top left.

What is the Physics Activity?

The Physics Activity lets you create objects, drop them, collide them, pin them into place or even link them together via joints and chains to build your own contraptions.

These contraptions are even shareable with others via the Share Activity function, but a little more about that later.

This next task is a little different from the others, just open the Physics Activity, start drawing shapes and have a play. Don't forget to have fun!

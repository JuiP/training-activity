Showing Text

Follow the step-by-step instructions below, or watch the video on the next page.

  1. Return to Turtle Blocks via the frame
  2. If your blocks are hidden, press the show blocks icon
  3. Click the media palette icon
  4. Drag a show block to the bottom of blocks
  5. Change to the word of your choice (eg Turtle!)
  6. Clear your canvas by pressing the eraser icon
  7. Press the snail and watch your turtle draw a green square, and then display the text you entered!
  8. Return to One Academy via keyboard or frame

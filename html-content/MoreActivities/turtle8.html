Displaying an Image and Saving

Follow the step-by-step instructions below, or watch the video on the next page.

  1. Return to Turtle Blocks via the frame
  2. If your blocks are hidden, press the show blocks icon
  3. Click the media items icon
  4. Drag an image block to the right of the show block, overwriting the text variable
  5. Click the image block and select a picture
  6. Clear your canvas by pressing the eraser icon
  7. Press the snail and watch your turtle draw a green square, and then display the image you picked!
  8. Press the turtle icon in the toolbar
  9. Press the save icon, and select save as image
  10. Stop Turtle Blocks and return to One Academy

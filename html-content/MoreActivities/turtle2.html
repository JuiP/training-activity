Drawing a Square in Turtle Blocks

Follow the step-by-step instructions below, or watch the video on the next page.

  1. From the Home view, open Turtle Blocks
  2. Drag a Forward block onto the canvas and click it to see the turtle move forward
  3. Drag a Right block and click it to see the turtle turn right
  4. Connect the Forward and Right blocks and click them to see the turtle go forward and turn right
  5. Connect the blocks to the Start block , and click the snail
  6. Press the Eraser and then click the snail 4 times !
  7. Use the Frame or keyboard to return to the Learn Sugar activity.

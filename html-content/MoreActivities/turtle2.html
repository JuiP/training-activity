Drawing a Square in Turtle Blocks

Follow the step-by-step instructions below, or watch the video on the next page.

  1. From the home view, open Turtle Blocks
  2. Drag a forward block onto the canvas and attach it below the start block
  3. Drag a right block and attach it below the forward block
  4. Continue by adding three more pairs of forward and right blocks
  5. Press the eraser to clear your canvas
  6. Press the snail and watch your turtle draw a square!
  7. Use the frame or keyboard to return to One Academy

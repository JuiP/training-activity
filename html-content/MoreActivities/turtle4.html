Drawing a Square using Repeat

Follow the step-by-step instructions below and look at the illustration on the next page.

  1. From the home view, open Turtle Blocks
  2. Drag the stack of Forward and Right blocks off of the Start block
  3. Press the Flow Palette icon
  4. Drag a Repeat block onto the canvas and attach it below the Start block
  5. Drag a Forward block and attach it in the Repeat block
  6. Drag a Right block and attach it below the Forward block
  7. Press the Eraser to clear your canvas
  8. Press the snail and watch your turtle draw a square!
  9. See what happens when you change the number of repeats and the degrees to turn right. Can you draw a hexagon?
  10. Use the Frame or keyboard to return to the Sugar Labs Academy Activity.

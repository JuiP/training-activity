What is Turtle Blocks?

Turtle Blocks is a deceptively straightforward art and graphics activity in which you use simple visual programming blocks to control the movement of a turtle . No coding is necessary, though the principles are the same.

Everywhere the turtle moves, a line is drawn behind.

By planning the movement of the turtle and the properties of the line, both simple or complex images can be drawn while you watch!

Opening the Frame

There are three ways to open the Frame:

  1. Press the Frame key on your keyboard. Pressing it again will close the Frame.
  2. Move your mouse to any corner of the screen. Doing so again will close the Frame.
  3. Swiping your finger down from the top edge of the screen. Repeating this action will close the Frame.

Try all three methods for opening and closing the Frame now.

You will be able to hit Next after you've done so.

Connecting to a Wi-Fi network

You can watch a video of how to connect on the next page, but here are the steps for your reference:

  1. Press the Network view button on your keyboard
  2. Hold the mouse over or click Wi-Fi symbols to see their names, and find your network
  3. Click a network and the Connect option will appear
  4. Press Connect!
  5. The network might ask for a key (password) to be entered - if you know it, enter it now and press OK!
  6. You should now be connected
  7. If you're not connected, there may be a proxy issue. Visit http://academy.one-education.org/proxies to learn more

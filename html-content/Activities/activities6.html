Make Speak Talk to You

Speak is a text-to-speech activity that kids love. Put text in the box, and the Speak robot will say it out loud! Some children will even get Speak to read books to them.

You can change how the robot looks and sounds, and even ask it basic questions.

In the next task you'll paste the text you copied from Write into Speak with Ctrl+V and get the robot to say it aloud. Then you'll ask the robot "Where is the capital of Australia?", change the look of the robot, describe your work to the Journal and finally Stop the Speak activity.

Insert a Picture into a Write Document

Write is Sugar's word processor. You can write, insert pictures, format text, create tables, and save documents as different kinds of files – just like any other word processor.

In the next task you'll open the Write Activity, insert the picture you took with Record , type a sentence about your picture, and make your sentence large and bold. Then you'll select and copy text with Ctrl+C!

Your Write document will be automatically saved to the Journal.

Hit Next to see how it's done and give it a go.

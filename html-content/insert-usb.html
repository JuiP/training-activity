Insert your USB stick

First up, you will need to plug your USB stick into the side of the XO.
If you're not sure how to do this, refresh your memory at:

http://academy.one-education.org

Your progress will be stored on this USB stick so that if you have to complete your training on another XO, all you need to do is plug in your USB stick to carry your progress over.

If you lose your USB stick, there will still be a copy of your progress on this XO - so don't worry.

Press Next to make your profile!

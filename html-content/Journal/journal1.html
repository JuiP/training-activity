Getting to Know the Journal

The Journal keeps a record of your activity in Sugar, it's where you find and organise your pictures, documents, files and the different things you've done in various activities.

When you describe what you've been doing in an activity, this is where it ends up - complete with your description so you can find that piece of work or play again.

The Journal is also able to read USB sticks that you have plugged in and is where you'll find files that you have saved from the Internet.

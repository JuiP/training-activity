Getting to Know the Journal

The Journal keeps a record of your actions within Sugar; it's where you find and organise your pictures, documents, files and the different things you've created in various Sugar Activities. You can search the Journal to revisit your earlier work.

The Journal is also where Sugar stores the descriptions of what you've been doing while using an Activity.

And from the Journal you are able to read from and write to USB sticks; and it's where you'll find files that you have downloaded from the Internet.

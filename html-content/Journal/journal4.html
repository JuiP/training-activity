Using the Portfolio

The Portfolio activity is a fun way to show off the work you've been doing on your XO.

By starring items in your Journal, you can then have them turned into a presentation via the Portfolio activity. You can then save that presentation as a PDF and show it to others!

In the next task you will star some of your work in the Journal, open the Portfolio, hit play and save the presentation as a PDF.

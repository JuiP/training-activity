Using the Portfolio

The Portfolio Activity is a fun way to show off the work you've been doing on your XO.

By starring items in your Journal, you can then have them turned into a presentation via the Portfolio Activity. You can then save that presentation as a PDF and share it with others!

In the next task you will star some of your work in the Journal, open the Portfolio, hit play to watch your presentation, and finally save it as a PDF.

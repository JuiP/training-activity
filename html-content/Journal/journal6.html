Copy your Presentation to USB

Now that you've made a presentation with Portfolio why don't we copy it onto a USB stick?

You should already have a USB stick plugged into the XO, so that part is taken care of.

In the next task you'll:

  • Open the Journal using the key;
  • Select the "My Portfolio" document you just created;
  • Then you'll press the Copy to USB button in the Toolbar;
  • Finally you'll check the USB to confirm that it copied by clicking the USB icon .

    • Hit Next to watch it happen before you try.

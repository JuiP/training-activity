Getting to Know the Views

Views are the different areas you access in Sugar. We've talked about them a little before, but now it's time to understand them.

Each view serves a purpose: to connect with friends, to find activities, to work on projects, etc.

Navigating views functions on the same principle as zooming in and out. Right now you're in the Activity view, but you could "zoom out" to the Home view, and "zoom out" from there to the Group and Neighbourhood views.

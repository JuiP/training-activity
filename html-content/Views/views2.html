The Four Views of Sugar

Activity View
This is where you are now. It's the view of the Activity you're currently using.

Home View
This is where you access Activities from, you see it whenever you press "My Turn".

Group View
Shows your frequent collaborators (friends) and their shared Activities.

Neighbourhood View
Shows nearby XOs and shared Activities on your network. It is also where you see available networks to join.

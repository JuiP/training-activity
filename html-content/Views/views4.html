Adding a Favourite to the Home View

The home view is where all your activity icons live. You can switch it between two different modes: Favourites and List.

List will show you all the available activities. Predictably it does so in the form of a list.

Favourites will show you only the activities that you tell it to. You are probably familiar with this mode as it's already set up, and likely showing activities in a spiral shape.

By going to list view, you can click the star next to an activity and add it to your favourites.

Have a Friend Join the Physics Activity

  1. Have your friend navigate to the neighbourhood view
  2. An icon with your XO colours, and a Physics activity icon below it should be present
  3. Have your friend press the Physics icon
  4. Have your friend select Join
  5. Congratulations, you're now sharing Physics with a friend!
  6. Draw and play with shapes together, and have twice as much fun!

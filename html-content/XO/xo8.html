Other Features

There are just a couple more features worth mentioning.

Sunlight Readable Screen
If you decrease the brightness of your screen to its lowest setting via the keyboard buttons , the XO screen will become high contrast and sunlight readable.

HDMI Out
If you look on the right side of your XO screen, underneath the antenna you'll find a micro-HDMI port. This port lets you plug your XO straight into a TV or projector via a mini-HDMI to standard-HDMI cable.

Other Features

There are a few more features worth mentioning.

Sunlight-Readable Screen
If you decrease the brightness of your screen to its lowest setting via the keyboard buttons , the XO screen will become high contrast black and white and sunlight readable.

HDMI Out
If you look on the right side of your XO screen, underneath the antenna you'll find a micro-HDMI port. This port lets you plug your XO straight into a TV or projector via a mini-HDMI to standard-HDMI cable.

Microphone, speaker, USB, and SD ports
Also under the antennas are microphone and speaker ports, and two USB ports. Under the base of the screen is an SD-card slot.
